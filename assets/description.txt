Open Gestures is the most minimal and efficient mouse gesture extension for Chrome, built for speed and privacy with no evil tracking code or spyware. With its robust radial detection system, you can perform 8 essential actions like Back/Forward, Scroll Top/Bottom, and complete Tab Management (Prev, Next, New, Close) with a single middle-button swipe. The extension features a real-time SVG trail, an action overlay for cursor feedback, and dynamic high-contrast color switching to ensure maximum visibility on any website. Fully customizable through a modern Material Design 3 menu and 100% open-source, Open Gestures provides a faster web experience that cannot be made smaller or faster.
