Open Gestures is the most minimal and efficient mouse gesture extension for Chrome, built for speed and privacy with no evil tracking code or spyware. With its robust radial detection system, you can perform 8 essential actions like Back/Forward, Scroll Top/Bottom, and complete Tab Management (Prev, Next, New, Close) with a single swipe of your preferred mouse button (Left, Middle, or Right). The extension features a real-time SVG trail, an action overlay for cursor feedback, and a customizable trail color to match your personal style. Fully customizable through a modern Material Design 3 menu and 100% open-source, Open Gestures provides a faster web experience that cannot be made smaller or faster.
